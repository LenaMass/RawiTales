<h1>About The Project</h1>
<p>
RawiTales combines language learning with cultural storytelling. 
Arabic-speaking learners can improve their English naturally 
through stories rooted in Saudi culture — making the learning 
experience both familiar and meaningful.
</p>
<p>
For international readers, the app offers a window into Saudi 
traditions, values, and heritage through engaging narrative — 
turning language learning into cultural discovery.
</p>

<h1>Features</h1>
<ul>
<li>Multiple story genres to choose from</li>
<li>Word translation — tap any word for instant meaning</li>
<li>Full story translations available</li>
<li>Reading progression tracking through stories</li>
<li>Word Bank — save words to revise later</li>
<li>Save a story to read later</li>
<li>VoiceOver accessibility support</li>
</ul>
